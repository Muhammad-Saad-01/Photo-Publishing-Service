Photo Publishing Service

This Service allows sellers on an ecommerce website to easily upload photos of their products and select a category for them. The service is powered by Springboot and has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for sellers to submit their photos. All submissions are then reviewed by an admin before being approved or declined, ensuring that only high-quality, relevant photos are displayed on the website. This can help improve the shopping experience for customers and allow sellers to effectively showcase their products. The admin also has the ability to review and approve or decline submissions, helping to ensure that only the best photos are displayed on the website.
